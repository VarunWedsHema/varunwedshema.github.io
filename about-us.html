ANCESTRY OUTLOOKS

Marriage is the most lovely ever cherished moment where two different families are made to walk
throughout rest of their lives alltogether. Here comes more about each individuals in our family

Thalayattumpillil Illom

It's one of 7 Ooranma family (owners) of the Poothrukovil Temple which is situated at Kottayam District in Kerala. Thalayattumpilli illom is the root of the big family tree which contains more than 100s of branches , Its more than 500 years old Nallukettu which includes sarppakavu ( Naaga Temple situated in a small forest ), mullakal Thevar and Vana durga Temple and the paradevatha prathista ( Bhagavathy ) in the North corner inside the Naalukettu which is known as Vadakkini.

Neelamana

Neelamana is one of the ancient illom originated from Karnataka and gradually got spread to north of Kerala. Ancestors of Neelamana parted it to different families and now in each corner of Kerala we can see different families of Neelamana. Among that, my illom is settled at Ramanatukara called as Thekethodi, which is lying in the lap of the Durga Devi temple. Now I am staying in my sweet home, Neelamana at Beypore with my lovely parents.
